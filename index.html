CLUB HYMN

The 'Cant del Barça', composed in 1974, is the Club's current anthem. It was written on the occasion of the 75th anniversary of the entity by the writers Josep M. Espinàs and Jaume Picas, with music by Manuel Valls, and is still fully current.
